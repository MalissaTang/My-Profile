Project Idea
ELift App

Elift app is an intelligent device app that informs company users about their elevator data and alerts the user if there may be a malfunction. Also, update on
policies and overheating details of maintenance and quality and power from the systems building's utility system data check. This app will help you see if you
need your lift systems to be cleaned and maintained to prevent accidents and see if everything is in place and completed before using it. Engineer's and maintenance policy checks need to be in place and officially signed by contractors.
This app will also include maintenance services, so users don't have to spend a lot of time researching to directly link you to someone in just a couple of clicks.

Details of the elevator being completed step by step

There will be a section where the user can click on a button, and a list of short steps will be provided to the user. The user can show this to maintenance services to keep up to date what has been done, on what date as well.
This app will prevent numerous accidents from happening; maintaining elevators in places can be a lot of work, but the safety of the people comes first. These accidents happen worldwide wherein in Chile, and a man caught in an elevator.
Suddenly the elevator malfunctioned and ascended 30 floors upwards in just 15 seconds with the elevator doors still open before he crashed into the shaft's roof, causing severe leg and head injuries. This elevator wasn't complete and included unintended car movement protection (rope brake) or any Overspeed governer.
